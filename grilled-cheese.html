Tasty Grilled Cheese

Description:

Meet the ULTIMATE comfort food: a crispy golden exterious thats a hug for your taste buds, with a warm cheesy secret inside. The age-old love story of bread and cheese, in a quick and easy recipe!

Ingredients

  • Sourdough Bread (You can use any kind, although I prefer sourdough.)
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Butter or Mayonaisse
  • OPTIONAL: Cooked Bacon

Steps:

  1. Using your butter or mayonaisse, spread and even layer on one side of the bread. The butter/mayo is what makes the bread crispy in the pan, so make sure the butter/mayo side of bread stays on the outside.
  2. On the side of bread that isnt buttered, place the cheddar cheese evenly on the surface. (If you are using bacon now is the time to add it to the sandwhich) Close the sandwhich.
  3. Heat a skillet/frying pan on medium heat (there is no need to add butter or oil to the pan, since we already buttered the bread). Place the sandwhich onto the skillet and let cook for roughly 5 minutes, or until the bread becomes golden brown and crispy. Flip and repeat.
  4. Finally, cut your sandwhich in half and ENJOY! For a more filling meal, you can serve with tomato soup to dip you sandwhich into, yum!
