Rice Krispie Treats Recipe

Description:

Rice Krispie Treats are the embodiment of simplicity in the kitchen. With just three base ingredients: crispy rice cereal, butter, and marshmallows - this recipe is a breeze for anyone to whip up. It's a delightful, no-fail treat that takes mere minutes to create, making it a perfect go-to for beginners, kids, or anyone looking for an effortless, sweet snack. These squares of marshmallow-infused joy are a universal favorite, proving that sometimes the easiest recipes yield the most delicious results!

Ingredients:

  1. Rice Krispies
  2. Marshmallows
  3. Butter
  4. Vanilla Extract

Steps:

  • Melt butter in a large saucepan on medium heat.
  • Add marshmallows to butter and stir until sticky and combined.
  • Add vanilla extract, stir, and add ricekrispies.
  • Stir rice crispies until all combined. Take off heat.
  • Add to greased 9x13 pan and spread until even.
  • Allow the treats to cool before cutting and removing from pan.
  • ENJOY!
