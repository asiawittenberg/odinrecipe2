Trail Mix Recipe

Description:

Trail mix, the adventurer's delight This simple yet satisfying snack is a symphony of flavors and textures that fuel your journey. A balanced blend of crunchy nuts, chewy dried fruits, and a hint of sweetness from chocolate chips or M&M's, it's your personal trailside treasure chest. A portable pick-me-up that's equally at home on a hike, in a lunchbox, or simply satisfying your snack cravings. Ready to fuel your every step with the perfect balance of energy, taste, and convenience

Ingredients:

  • Peanuts
  • Cashews
  • Raisins
  • M&M's or chcolatte chips
  • Pumpkin Seeds
  • Almonds

Steps:

  1. Add all ingredients to a bowl. Mix and serve.
  2. ENJOY!
